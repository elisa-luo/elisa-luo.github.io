Very broadly speaking, I do security measurement research. Currently, I'm working on some web privacy and LLM stuff. I've also worked on several projects in the enterprise security space, as well as with crypto hacks. I'm also serving as the Publicity Chair for UCSD's Graduate Women in Computing (GradWIC) Group!
